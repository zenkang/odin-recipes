Sautéed Mushrooms

Description

Sautéed Mushrooms are a simple and delicious side dish that pairs perfectly with steak, chicken, or any main course. These tender mushrooms are cooked in butter and herbs for maximum flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound mixed mushrooms (button, cremini, or shiitake), sliced
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup dry white wine
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme leaves
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add sliced onions and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add mushrooms to the skillet and cook without stirring for 3-4 minutes to allow browning.
  4. Stir mushrooms and continue cooking until they release their moisture and become golden brown, about 5-6 minutes.
  5. Add garlic and thyme, cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  6. Pour in white wine and let it simmer until reduced by half, about 2 minutes.
  7. Add remaining butter and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
