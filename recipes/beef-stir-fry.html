Beef Stir-Fry

Description

Beef Stir-Fry is a quick and flavorful Asian-inspired dish loaded with tender beef and crisp vegetables. Perfect for busy weeknights when you want something healthy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin, cut into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, minced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, and cornstarch. Set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
  3. Add beef strips and stir-fry until browned, about 3-4 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  4. Add remaining oil to the wok. Add onions and bell peppers, stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Add broccoli and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until crisp-tender.
  6. Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  7. Return beef to the wok and add the sauce mixture.
  8. Stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until sauce thickens and coats everything.
  9. Drizzle with sesame oil and garnish with green onions before serving.
